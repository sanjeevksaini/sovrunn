# RFC-0023: Decision and Audit Standard

See `docs/architecture/decision-and-audit-standard.md`.

## Decision

All major Sovrunn decisions must use structured decision objects with reason codes, human-readable reasons, alternatives, policy references, suggested actions, and audit linkage.
